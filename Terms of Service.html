Description of Service

DOA is a desktop application that applies real-time GPU shader-based visual correction to your screen. It is designed for users with refractive errors who want clearer screen readability without wearing glasses. The App provides:

  • Screen-wide vision correction for myopia, hyperopia, astigmatism, and presbyopia
  • GPU-accelerated overlay rendering at up to 60fps
  • Visual filters including blue light reduction and color blindness correction modes
  • Multi-monitor support (Premium tier)
  • Prescription management and cloud sync via Firebase

Important: DOA is not a medical device and is not regulated as one. It is not intended to replace prescription glasses, contact lenses, or any professional eye care treatment. If you experience eye strain, headaches, blurred vision, or discomfort while using DOA, stop use immediately and consult a qualified eye care professional. Regular eye examinations remain necessary regardless of DOA usage.

DOA makes no claim that its correction is medically equivalent to wearing prescription eyewear.
