Common Questions

DOA applies a refractive correction layer to your display. Our algorithms process the screen in real-time—predistorting the image so that when it hits your eye, it appears perfectly crisp, effectively "undoing" the blur caused by myopia or astigmatism.

DOA supports Myopia (up to -6.00D), Hyperopia (up to +6.00D), and Astigmatism. The effectiveness depends on your monitor's pixel density—high DPI (4K) screens offer the highest range of correction.

We use GPU-accelerated shaders to keep latency under 30-50ms. This is imperceptible for office work, coding, and browsing. We do recommend disabling it for competitive fast-twitch gaming.
