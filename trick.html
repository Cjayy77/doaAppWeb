Important Update for Personal Developer Accounts

Scheduled App Store Service Pause

Please note that App Store services will be temporarily paused from June 28 to November 12 due to preparations for the launch of iOS 19 and new iPhone models. We recommend planning submissions and updates accordingly.

Effective Immediately

In order to maintain a secure and trusted App Store experience, we are updating our policies regarding payment capabilities in apps published by personal developer accounts.

Whatâ€™s changing:

  • Personal developer accounts are no longer permitted to offer any form of in-app payment or external payment processing.
  • Apps currently offering payment functionality under a personal account must transition to an Organization account to remain compliant.
  • New apps submitted by personal accounts with payment functionality will be rejected.
  • Developers who intend to offer payment capabilities must enroll in the Apple Developer Program as an Organization.

What you need to do:

If you currently have an app on the App Store that includes payments and is registered under a personal account:

  • Convert your Apple Developer account to an Organization account.
  • Update your app information accordingly in App Store Connect.
  • Resubmit your app for review.

To create a new Organization account, visit developer.apple.com/enroll.

For detailed instructions on converting your account or to get support, please see our Account Help.

Thank you for helping us ensure a safe, high-quality experience for users around the world.
