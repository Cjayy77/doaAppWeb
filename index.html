Frequently Asked Questions

How does DOA work?

DOA applies a specialized digital correction layer to your screen that replicates the effects of your prescription glasses, allowing you to see clearly without wearing them. Our advanced algorithms process the display in real-time to provide optimal clarity.

Will it work with my prescription?

DOA supports a wide range of prescriptions including myopia (up to -6.00D), hyperopia (up to +4.00D), and astigmatism (up to -2.50D). For prescriptions outside these ranges, please contact our support team for personalized assistance.

Can I still use the app after my 3 hours passed?

Yes! You are free to continue using the app and its services like the web browser and the media player even if your 3-hour correction limit is reached. For more correction time, you can upgrade to premium

Does DOA work with all devices?

DOA is compatible with most modern smartphones, tablets, and computers running iOS 14+, Android 10+, Windows 10+, and macOS 11+. Support for all the mentionned devices will be added gradually.

Is my prescription data secure?

Absolutely! We take your privacy seriously. All prescription data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols and stored securely. We never share your personal or medical information with third parties.

Why is the correction effect limited only within the app?

Due to platform restrictions imposed by Apple and Android, the correction effect can only be applied within specific applications rather than system-wide. These limitations are set by the operating system and cannot be bypassed. Unless Apple and Android update their policies to allow broader implementation, this restriction will remain in place.
