Frequently Asked Questions

How does DOA work?

DOA applies a specialized digital correction layer to your screen that adjusts the display based on your prescription, allowing you to see clearly without wearing glasses. Our advanced algorithms process the display in real-time to provide optimal clarity.

Will it work with my prescription?

DOA supports a wide range of prescriptions including myopia (up to -6.00D), hyperopia (up to +4.00D), and astigmatism (up to -2.50D). For prescriptions outside these ranges, please contact our support team for personalized assistance.

Can I still use the app after my 3 hours passed?

Yes! You are free to continue using the app and its services like the web browser and the media player even if your 3-hour limit is reached.

Does DOA work with all devices?

DOA is compatible with most modern smartphones, tablets, and computers running iOS 14+, Android 10+, Windows 10+, and macOS 11+. Check our compatibility page for a full list of supported devices.

Is my prescription data secure?

Absolutely! We take your privacy seriously. All prescription data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols and stored securely. We never share your personal or medical information with third parties.
